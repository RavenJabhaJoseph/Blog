Tell me about
yourself ?

T his is the most prominent question asked after a candidate enters the room or after looked into the resume. Here are some innovative tips which makes your self introduction different and unique from others.

  • Don’t tell about your family and related details (HR is not interested in knowing that).
  • Have a positive introduction.
  • Add your strengths with short example (keep it short).
  • Tell which is not in the resume.
  • Grab HR’s attention by unique hobbies.
  • Showcase your best skills with suitable example.
